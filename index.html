About RAIN Lab

RAIN Lab is a research group at Lebanese American University focused on advancing the state of the art in reasoning, artificial intelligence, and natural language processing. Our work explores fundamental questions about how machines can understand, reason about, and generate human language.

We develop novel methods and models that push the boundaries of what's possible in AI, with applications ranging from question answering and logical inference to dialogue systems and knowledge representation.
